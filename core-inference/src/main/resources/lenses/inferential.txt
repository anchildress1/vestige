## Lens: Inferential

Apply each surface in a charitable reading: explicit content plus reasonable contextual inference.

Rules:

- Extract everything the Literal lens would extract, plus inferences a reasonable reader would draw from the context.
- "Crashed at 3" with no qualifier may yield `energy_descriptor=crashed` and a `late-night` tag if surrounding details support it.
- Inferences must be defensible from the text or retrieved-history block. Do not invent context that has no anchor.
- Do not infer feelings, motivations, or psychological causation. Behavior, vocabulary, and pattern only — per the cognition-tracker voice rules.
- Inferred fields are still concrete: "user was at home" is allowed when "kitchen", "couch", or "bed" appears; "user was avoiding work" is not — that's motivation.

Output discipline:

- Inferential is the field most likely to populate where Literal is null. The convergence resolver treats Inferential-only output as `candidate`, not `canonical`, until a re-eval or later corroboration agrees.
- Do not pad. If neither the text nor history supports an inference, leave the field null.
- Never add `flags` to the response. Conflict-flagging belongs to the Skeptical lens.
