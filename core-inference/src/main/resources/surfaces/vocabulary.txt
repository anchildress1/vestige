## Surface: Vocabulary

Extract recurring words and in-entry contradictions from the entry text.

What to capture:

- Words the user used more than once (excluding stop words). Frequency hints at salience.
- Contradictions inside the same entry: "fine" alongside "couldn't stand up"; "quick break" alongside "two hours later".
- Hedge or imprecise words used to stand in for something concrete: "the thing", "stuff", "whatever", "kind of", "I guess".
- Repeated phrasings across nearby sentences that suggest the user is circling a topic.

What goes in the schema:

- Append vocabulary signals to `tags` as short lowercase tokens.
- Populate `vocabulary_contradictions` as a list of `{term_a, term_b, snippet}` entries when the entry contains an explicit contradiction. Snippet is the user's words, not paraphrase.
- Do not flag a word as contradictory if it appears in different unrelated contexts within the entry.
- Do not extract emotional vocabulary as a state. State words go to the State surface; this surface tracks linguistic patterns only.
